Touch Angle Measurement Tool

Measure angles by placing points on your camera feed

How to Use:

  1. Allow camera access when prompted
  2. Touch the camera feed to place points (minimum 3 points)
  3. Points will remain until you clear them
  4. Click "Start Recording" to capture data for 1 second
  5. Use "Save Data" to download your measurements
